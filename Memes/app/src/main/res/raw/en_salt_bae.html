Salt Bae

Nusret Gökçe, also known as Salt Bae, (born in 1983) is a Turkish chef who owns Nusr-Et. His art of cooking and preparing meat became an internet sensation.

Gökçe became more widely known via the internet during January 2017 for the way he "suavely" cuts his meat. When asked about his favourite meat he jokingly said he was a vegetarian.

His fame came from one viral video named: "Ottoman Steak", which was posted on the 7th of January, 2017 with his restaurant Twitter account Nusret. It was viewed 10 million times on Instagram after which he was dubbed "Salt Bae" because of his iconic sprinkle of salt. He was later interviewed by NBC News, where he said "The shape of the meat and the taste of it starting from the top down is a part of me... All of my feelings are coming from inside of the meat down to when I put the salt onto the meat."
